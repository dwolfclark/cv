Daniel Wolf-Clark

Business Scaling, Product Leadership, Consultant, Speaker

Having grown up in a family of passionate educators and entrepreneurs, it was a natural progression that led Daniel to a range of leadership roles within the education and technology sectors. As Head of Product and CEO of the award winning company myEdOnline, he and his team provided services and helped over 1100+ organisations across Australia and internationally better use technology for learning. Daniel is passionate about making a positive difference in the world and driven to work with and contribute to organisations that seek to do this at scale. He believes that empathy is a key attribute of designing amazing products and building successful businesses. Drawing upon a wide range of skills developed across multiple industries, Daniel shares his knowledge, love of technology, data, automation and experience working in cross-functional roles, to help businesses maximise their scalability and success. He also loves sport, playing and coaching basketball, and getting involved in social causes he is passionate about.
